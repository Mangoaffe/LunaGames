Privacy Policy for Luna Games Mobile Applications

Key Point: Luna Games does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data stays on your device. We only use AdMob for advertisements.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

Luna Games does NOT collect any personal information including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Game progress, scores, or achievements
  • Device identifiers (except those used by AdMob)
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Any user-generated content

All your game data remains locally stored on your device only.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our games display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising Identifier: A unique, user-resettable ID for advertising purposes
  • IP Address: For general location and fraud prevention
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • App Usage: Which ads are displayed and whether they are clicked

This data is collected by Google, not by Luna Games.

3. How Ads Work in Our Games

Advertisements in our games are:

  • Non-personalized: We do not enable personalized advertising
  • Age-appropriate: Suitable for all audiences including children
  • Optional: You can play our games without viewing ads
  • Safe: All ads go through Google's content review process

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our games are designed to be safe for children:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information from children of any age
  • Only non-personalized, family-friendly ads are shown
  • No chat features or social interactions
  • No in-app purchases that require personal information

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there are no security risks related to personal information on our end. Your game progress is stored locally on your device and is as secure as your device.

6. Your Control Over Data

You can:

  • Reset Advertising ID: Clear your advertising identifier in device settings
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Disable ad personalization in your device settings
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local game data
  • Play Offline: Our games work without internet connection
